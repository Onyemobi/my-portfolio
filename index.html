I am Onyemobi Anyiwo

About me

A versatile professional with over a decade of experience helping businesses and individuals achieve their goals. My background includes driving online retail growth for major brands like Abercrombie & Fitch, Express, and Big Lots. I leverage this extensive experience to provide comprehensive services in E-commerce, Digital Marketing, Tech Training, Application Development and more. Whether you're looking to launch an online store, streamline your non-profit's operations, or advance your career with one-on-one mentorship, I'm dedicated to empowering you with the tools and strategies for success.

My Services

E-Commerce

Whether you're just starting your online store or looking to boost your current sales, I'm here to help. I specialize in setting up and optimizing e-commerce shops on platforms like Etsy, eBay, and Gumroad. I'll handle everything from the initial setup to fine-tuning your listings, so you can focus on your products and customers.

Tech Training

I offer personalized training to help you master essential tools. I provide individual and group training sessions on how to leverage the power of AI with platforms like Google Gemini and OpenAI's ChatGPT to boost your productivity. I also offer comprehensive training on G-Suite (now Google Workspace) and Microsoft Office to help you become more efficient with everyday applications like Word, Excel, and Gmail.

Digital Marketing

Ready to build a stronger online presence? I can help you create and redesign professional websites that capture your brand and engage your audience. I also offer email marketing services to help you connect directly with customers, as well as SEO (Search Engine Optimization) to improve your visibility and drive more organic traffic to your site.

Non-Profit Consulting

I help non-profit organizations modernize and streamline their operations to better serve their mission. I can guide you through the process of obtaining and properly utilizing 501(c)(3) status, ensuring your organization is compliant and maximizing its tax-exempt benefits. Additionally, I'll help you adopt and integrate modern tools to improve efficiency, from administrative tasks to fundraising and donor management, so you can focus on making a greater impact.

Career Mentorship

Starting a new career or making a transition can be challenging. I provide personalized guidance to help you navigate your professional journey, whether you're just starting out or making a major change. From building a standout resume to preparing for interviews and identifying your next steps, I'll help you gain the confidence and clarity you need to achieve your career goals.

Application Development

Need a custom application to streamline your operations? I create and customize powerful applications for businesses and organizations, specializing in platforms like Salesforce and other CRMs (Customer Relationship Management) systems. Whether you need a new app built from the ground up or a tool tailored to fit your existing workflow, I'll develop a solution that helps you work smarter and more efficiently.
